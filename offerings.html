What does DevilSec offer?

1 on 1 Mentoring

DevilSec can provide hands on training from senior level members, some of whom already work in the info sec industry.

Attend a meeting in person or reach out on Discord using one of the server's many channels to get the appropriate support you need.

Intro to Linux and Command Line

Several staff members use Linux as their daily driver and can provide advice and suggestions on how to improve.

Some members started with zero knowledge of Linux and the terminal and were able to learn and apply their skills right away.

Project Based Learning Opportunities

DevilSec continues to grow! Staff need volunteers when it comes to building CTF infrastructure, educational materials, and other web based technologies.

There are new projects every year and all members are welcome to join projects and learn along the way.

Guest Speakers

We find industry professionals to give presentations on their projects, work, and how they got into their fields.

These talks provide opportunities for Q&A and getting advice on networking and bolstering your professional portfolio. These speaking events are usually are most popular ones to attend!

Networking

Whether you are a virtual participant or an in-person member, DevilSec includes working professionals who regularly post job openings and internship opportunities.

On campus events provide a way to introduce yourself and get more involved while virtual meetings and discussions can help other members get to know you better and offer advice based on your interests.

Community

Our group enjoys learning and teaching new tools, playing Hack the Box as a group, and just chatting in voice channels in Discord.

We encourage a welcoming atmosphere so anyone at any level regardless of their background can join and start learning at their own pace while having access to support when they need it.
